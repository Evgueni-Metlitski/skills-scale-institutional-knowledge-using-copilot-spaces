# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, coach the team on agile practices, and remove impediments. They work to ensure the team can deliver iteratively and continuously improve their processes.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate agile ceremonies (daily standups, sprint planning, reviews, retrospectives)
- Remove blockers and impediments that slow the team
- Coach the team on agile practices and self-organization
- Shield the team from external distractions
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement
- Track team velocity and process metrics

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and flow
- Foster team collaboration and self-organization
- Ensure agile principles are followed
- Drive continuous process improvement

### Typical Communication
- Facilitate daily standups and all agile ceremonies
- Collaborate with PM on project planning and risk management
- Partner with PdM on backlog refinement and prioritization
- Coach Developers on agile practices and sprint execution
- Escalate systemic impediments to leadership

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers create intuitive and delightful user experiences. They research user needs, design interfaces, create prototypes, and collaborate with Product Managers and Developers to deliver user-centered solutions.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and high-fidelity designs
- Develop and maintain design systems and style guides
- Collaborate with PdM to refine requirements and user stories
- Partner with Developers to ensure design implementation fidelity
- Validate designs through user testing and feedback

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible, and delightful user experiences
- Ensure design consistency across the product
- Validate designs with real user feedback
- Balance user needs with technical constraints

### Typical Communication
- Weekly design reviews with PdM and stakeholders
- Design handoff sessions with Developers
- User testing sessions and research findings presentations
- Collaborate in sprint planning to scope design work
- Participate in retrospectives to improve design-dev collaboration

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business needs and technical solutions. They gather and document requirements, create detailed user stories, and ensure that solutions meet business objectives.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements
- Facilitate requirements workshops with stakeholders
- Create detailed user stories with acceptance criteria
- Analyze and document business processes and workflows
- Support PdM in backlog refinement and prioritization
- Validate that delivered solutions meet business needs

### Goals
- Ensure technical solutions address actual business needs
- Translate complex business requirements into actionable stories
- Maintain clear traceability from requirements to delivery
- Support data-driven decision making with analysis

### Typical Communication
- Requirements workshops with stakeholders and subject matter experts
- Collaborate with PdM on product vision and backlog prioritization
- Work with Developers to clarify requirements and acceptance criteria
- Partner with QA/Testers on acceptance testing
- Present analysis findings and recommendations to leadership

---

## QA/Tester

### Role Summary
QA/Testers ensure product quality through comprehensive testing strategies. They develop test plans, execute manual and automated tests, identify defects early, and validate that features meet acceptance criteria before release.

### Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain test plans and test cases
- Execute manual and automated testing
- Identify, document, and track defects
- Collaborate with Developers on test automation and quality practices
- Validate features against acceptance criteria
- Report on quality metrics and release readiness
- Perform regression testing before releases

### Goals
- Ensure high product quality and reliability
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Increase test automation coverage
- Provide clear visibility into quality and release readiness

### Typical Communication
- Daily collaboration with Developers on bug triage
- Partner with BA on acceptance criteria and test scenarios
- Report quality metrics to PM and stakeholders
- Participate in sprint planning to estimate testing effort
- Contribute to retrospectives on quality improvements

---

## Stakeholder

### Role Summary
Stakeholders represent the interests of specific business functions, user groups, or sponsors. They provide strategic input, review deliverables at key milestones, and ensure that projects align with broader organizational goals.

### Responsibilities
- Provide business context and strategic direction
- Review and approve key project deliverables
- Participate in requirements gathering and validation
- Champion the project within their organization
- Provide feedback on demos and prototypes
- Make or influence go/no-go decisions at project gates

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver expected business value
- Align project outcomes with organizational strategy
- Represent the needs of their business unit or user group
- Make informed decisions on priorities and trade-offs

### Typical Communication
- Monthly project status updates from PM
- Milestone reviews and demos
- Participate in project initiation and planning sessions
- Provide feedback through PdM on product direction
- Escalation point for critical decisions or issues
